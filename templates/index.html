Welcome to Smart Attendance

This system uses advanced face recognition technology to automatically mark attendance. Simply stand in front of the camera and your attendance will be recorded instantly.

How to Use:

  1. Ensure your face images are in the dataset/YourName/ folder
  2. Run python encode_faces.py to encode your face
  3. Run python camera.py to start the attendance system
  4. Face the camera - your attendance will be marked automatically
  5. Press Enter to exit the camera
